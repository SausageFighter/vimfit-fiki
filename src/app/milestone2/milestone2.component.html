Idea Validation

The idea validation is one of the most important stages when creating a start-up. We have questioned more than 230 persons using Google Forms questionnaires, aged between 14 and 50 years, males and females, certified trainers, and regular persons. We have collected their feedback, we had conversations beside the questionnaires, we listened to their stories and hopefully, we understood their necessities, pains, and desires.
