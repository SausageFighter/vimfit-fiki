Personas

As we have two mainly types of users, we will present two personas.

Trainee

Meet Alex. She is from London and she is 28 yo. she is a vet doctor, she likes being around people and animals and she is a party animal. Her childhood dream was to be a storyteller, so right now she is working on a book.

A regular day for Alex looks like this: she wakes up at 7 am, she has her coffee and breakfast, and at 8 am she works at the vet shop. She doesn't usually have much activity in the shop, so she mostly browses the web for funny content. She leaves the shop at 5 pm, then goes shopping for fresh food in the local market. After cooking dinner for her and her two cats, she continues writing her book. She falls asleep around 12:30 am.

Alex thinks about being more physically active, but can't find the time and motivation to do so. After she started her book, she also gained some weight and she feels very self-aware because of that. She is planning on releasing the book in two months, so she prioritizes finishing it.

Sometimes, Alex thinks about her age and how she's getting older and older and how every day a new part of her body starts hurting. The lack of physical activity doesn't help eighter, as she has frustration built up and she's on the verge of falling into depression.

Problems:

  • lack of time and motivation to work out

Needs:

  • professional help for her physical and mental health
  • boosting her self-esteem
Personal Trainer

Meet Adam. He is also from London and he is 35 yo. He is a personal trainer for 10 years and started making youtube videos. His childhood dream was to become a professional tennis player, but he hurt his arm in a competition and he couldn't practice like before, so right now he trains others to be the best versions of themselves.

2-hours typical day for Adam is to wake up at 5 am, have a sprint around the neighborhood, and meditate in Hyde Park until 7 am. After that, he goes home, has breakfast, goes to the gym, and starts working with clients or for himself. He has a 2-hour break at the gym to edit or make videos for his channel. Recently he hit the 2k subscribers so he tries his best to create good quality content. After he leaves the gym around 6 pm, he visits his mother and cooks dinner for her. He is usually asleep around 10 pm.

In the last year, Adam's mother found out that she had breast cancer, but the good news is that it can be treated. Since then, Adam has tried to earn money by working extra hours. He's thinking about taking a second job but doesn't have the time to do that and take care of his mother at the same time.

After his accident, Adam promised to himself that he will empower other people to fulfill their dreams, so that was the reason behind starting a Youtube channel. He want to reach as many people as he can, but he grows very slow, and can't reach each person one by one, but as a whole.

Problems:

  • lack of money

Needs:

  • a big audience
  • reaching people individually
