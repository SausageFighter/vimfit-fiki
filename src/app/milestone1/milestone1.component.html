VIMFit - your daily fitness motivation

Problem

The lack of education (TBD) from health care professionals to end clients/patients amounts to the main decision whether to start a healthy lifestyle or not.

There is no free platform where “professional lifestyle coaches” can enable a population to start making healthier life choices.

While COVID is making a rampant change in the world economy, one of the industries suffering the most is the fitness industry. With gyms closing down and re-opening for short periods of time before another spike hits. People are looking for new ways to keep or get in shape from home or in a safe environment with the guidance of a professional lifestyle coach.

Solution

Habitual learning at its peak with input from health professionals from all over the world.

Free guidance and support from a community with a global outreach.

During COVID with adults spending more time at home, VIMFit will be taking this opportunity to entice new users to stay active and healthy with mass health analysis triggered by country, age, ethnic background, or gender.
